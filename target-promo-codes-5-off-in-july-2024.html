FAQs

Does Target offer free shipping?

Yes, when you spend over $35 or use your Target RedCard, you’ll automatically qualify for free shipping which typically takes up to two working days to arrive. Alternatively, you can also opt to pick up your order from your local store or have it brought out to your car.

Does Target offer a military discount?

Yes, at Target, Members of Target Circle receive 10% off two qualifying storewide purchases as a tribute to military, veterans, and families. You’ll also get access to hundreds of deals and offers, an exclusive 5% discount on your birthday, 1% earnings when you shop online or in-store, and loads more.  

Can I shop the Target sale?

Yes, you can often find discounts of up to 40% and you can shop by categories including Baby Clothing, Beauty and Personal Care, Home, and Clothing and Accessories. Target also runs sale events during seasonal periods such as Christmas, Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and Easter. 

Can I sign up for the Target email newsletter?

Yes, simply enter your email address into the email address pop-up box at the footer of the Target website and watch as the deals arrive directly into your inbox. It’s a great way to stay up to date about the latest Target coupon codes, early access to sale events, product launches, and loads more. 

What is the Target Redcard?

The Redcard is a debit or credit card from Target. If you sign up for a RedCard, you can save 5% on orders every day. You’ll also get 2-day free shipping with each $35 purchase at Target. 

Does Target have an app?

Yes. Target does have an app. Sign up to Target and add this app to your phone for exclusive savings. You can sign up to Target Circle with your preferred email address for free.  

Does Target price match?

Target does price match for products bought within the past 14 days. Your item will need to be in stock from a select list of retailers or at Target with a lower price. You can check the Target Price Guarantee on their website for more information.  

Does Target accept returns?

Yes, Target accepts returns made within 90 days of the date of purchase. If you’re a RedCard holder, you’ll have an additional 30 days to return your order for a full refund. For Target-owned brands and registry items, you have up to a year to change your mind. We recommend heading to the Target ‘Returns’ page for more information.

Hints and tips

Shop the sale: Whether you’re looking to save some money on your Target order, or you love finding the best bargains, shopping the sale is a great place to start. Either head to the ‘Deals’ or ‘Clearance’ page to make some savings. Their deals are updated regularly and are often dependent on the season, and hundreds of items are added to the clearance daily.  

Sign Up for the Newsletter - You can sign up for the Target newsletter for free and get updates on sales and special deals. They might also send you exclusive offers and promo codes. To join, visit the Target website and scroll to the bottom of the homepage to enter your email address. 

Join Target Circle: Joining Target’s rewards program is an excellent way to get extra perks and deals every time you shop. Target Circle gives you 1% back on all your earnings, as well as early access to hundreds of offers on your favorite brands and a 5% off birthday gift. Earn in-store by entering your mobile number or scanning your wallet, online by checking out with your Target.com account, or by entering your receipt on the dashboard if you forgot to scan.

Get a RedCard: Apply for a RedCard from Target to save more in-store and online. A Target RedCard will see you save 5% on all purchases, subject to condition. Choose from a credit card, debit card, or a reloadable account. Pay no monthly or annual fees, get free 2-day shipping on thousands of items across the Target online store, and get an additional 30 days of returns or exchanges.

Download the Target app: Download the Target app on the App Store or Google Play Store for an easy, personalized shopping experience. Enjoy a variety of perks including a shopping list feature, free Drive Up delivery, access to same-day delivery and Target Circle offers, and early access to the latest sales and promotions. What’s more, enjoy purchasing all of your everyday essentials from the comfort of your own home, with just a click of a button.  

Follow Target on Social Media - You can follow Target on their social media if you prefer to avoid newsletter emails. This is a good way to keep an eye out for sales and the latest trends. You can follow Target on Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter. Simply scroll to the footer of their website for their social media links. 

Target Registry Discounts - Target lets you create a Gift Registry for your baby or wedding event, which you can share with others. When your event date is near, they will send you a 15% off coupon to use on any items still on your list. You can use this coupon twice and across their entire store. 

Target Furniture Discounts - Are you looking to update your interior design on a budget? Target has furniture discounts and offers throughout the year. Previously, these have included up to 20% off Kitchen and Dining items. You can shop popular brands too such as Threshold Designed with Studio McGee.
