Pop Power Couple Jordin Sparks and Jason Derulo Bet on Who Will Win

 Posted on August 3, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  200 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

One-half of pop power couple Jason Derulo and Jordin Sparks may find him- or herself sleeping on the couch tomorrow night.

After American Idol’s finale performance show on Tuesday, Derulo revealed that he is a Jessica Sanchez fan and Sparks, who won season 6 of Idol, is rooting for Phillip Phillips.

“We have a bet,” he told reporters after the show. “If she wins, I have to cook her a nice meal.” Doesn’t sound so challenging, unless you’re Jason Derulo. “I can’t even cook a hot dog, let alone an actual meal,” he cracked.

And if Sanchez — and by proxy Derulo — wins? “Jordin has to give me a 10-minute foot massage,” he said. “And she hates feet.”

Derulo was a semi-regular presence on Idol this season thanks to Coca-Cola’s “Perfect Harmony” program, which asked fans of the show to help Derulo complete the song “Undefeated.”

He performed the completed number on Idol’s Tuesday’s show, but said afterwards that he didn’t get to see the rest of the Sanchez-Phillips showdown.

The two finalists sang three songs each on Tuesday’s show, including their own individual coronation numbers.
