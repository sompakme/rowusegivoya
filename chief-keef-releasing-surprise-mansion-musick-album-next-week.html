Chief Keef Releasing Surprise &quot;Mansion Musick&quot; Album Next Week

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  272 words  |  Martina Birk

It doesn't seem like Chief Keef is really all that interested in promoting his own music, as yet another one of his projects has appeared on iTunes with no word from him. 

His last project, the Ottopsy EP, was also spoiled in a similar manner, and less than a month later, his brand new album Mansion Musick has appeared in the iTunes store available for pre-order.

So far, the only track we've heard from the project is Keef's recent single, "Part Ways," but there's plenty more in store for Chief Keef fans. The project is 11 tracks song, and relatively feature free. With the exception being "Uh Uh," which will feature Playboi Carti. The two had recently collaborated on Carti's track "Mileage" off his sophomore Die Lit album. Some of the song titles here also promise something interesting, such as the opening track "Belieber."

Real Chief Keef fans are probably shaking their heads in confusion right now, as the name Mansion Musick should already be very familiar to them. Way back in 2014, Chief Keef actually released a mixtape with the exact same title as his new album. Why he chose to do this instead of simply adding a "2" on the end of it is unclear, but Keef surely has his reasons for doing so. 

The album is set to drop next Friday, July 13th, which hopefully wont be a bad omen for the album's performance. Check out the full tracklist for the project below.

1. Belieber
2. Rawlings
3. TV On (Big Boss)
4. Uh Uh Feat. Playboi Carti
5. Sky Say
6. Hand Made
7. Get This Money
8. Yet
9. Part Ways
10. Tragedies
11. Letter
