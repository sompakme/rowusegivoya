Raquel Rodriguez Wins Last Chance Battle Royal on 2/19 WWE RAW to Earn Elimination Chamber Spot

 Posted on August 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  287 words  |  Aldo Pusey

WWE is headed to Perth, Australia for its last PLE before WrestleMania 40, Elimination Chamber with two high-stakes chamber matches scheduled to happen, both for the Men and Women.

While the Men’s chamber match slots were filled last week on SmackDown, there was one coveted spot left in the Women’s chamber match, and a last-chance Battle Royal was held on this week’s episode of RAW.

The over-the-top-rope melee featured the likes of Shyana Baszler, Zoey Stark, Zelina Vega, Chelsea Green, Michin, and Alba Fyre, all of whom failed to qualify for the aforementioned match in their previous attempts. Moreover, the match also saw the return of the dominant Raquel Rodriguez after months of being on the sidelines.

Each of the women’s division competitors went at each other, with everything they had and a golden opportunity hanging in the balance. The battle royal came down to Raquel Rodriguez and allies, Zoey Stark and Shayna Baszler, but it was the Latina who tossed both of them out in back-to-back succession.

While at the moment, she believed that she won, Chelsea Green emerged from behind, not being eliminated from the match, and tried to capitalize on the same. But Rodriguez overpowered her and tossed her out of the ring to win the bout.

With her impressive win, Raque Rodriguez now joins Becky Lynch, Bianca Belair, Liv Morgan, Naomi, and Tiffany Stratton in the Women’s Elimination Chamber match. The winner of the bout would earn a WWE Women’s World title against the dominant champion, Rhea Ripley at WrestleMania 40.
