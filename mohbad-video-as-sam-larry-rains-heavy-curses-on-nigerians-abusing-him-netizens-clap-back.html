Mohbad: Video As Sam Larry Rains Heavy Curses on Nigerians Abusing Him, Netizens Clap Back

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  393 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Sam Larry has fired back at Nigerians who are flooding his Instagram page with negative comments over Mohbad's death
  • In a video that has since gone viral, the Lagos-based socialite countered the heavy curses directed at him
  • Sam Larry's recent response comes a few days after he forgot to lock his comment section while celebrating Marlian signee Zinoleesky

Popular socialite Samson Balogun, better known as Sam Larry, is trending on social media over a video of him clapping back at those dragging him over his alleged involvement in Mohbad's death.

Recall that ever since Sam Larry was released from detention alongside Marlian label boss Naira Marley, Nigerians have repeatedly insulted the duo at every given opportunity.

Sam Larry curses trolls

The socialite, who is fed up with the barrage of insults hurled at him, abused the trolls. He said they had refused to let him be over a case he knew nothing about.
