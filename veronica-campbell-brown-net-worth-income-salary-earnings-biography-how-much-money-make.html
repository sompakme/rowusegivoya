Veronica Campbell-Brown C.D (born 15 May 1982) is a Jamaican sprinter who competes in the 100 and 200 metres. She is a member of the Jamaican national track and field team. In addition to being a seven-time Olympic medalist, she is just the second woman in history to win two consecutive Olympic 200 m races, after Bärbel Wöckel of Germany in the 1976 and 1980 Games. Campbell-Brown is one of just nine athletes in history to have won world championships in the same sports event at the youth, junior, and senior levels. 

Personal bests in the 100 m and 200 m are 10.76 seconds for her and 21.74 seconds for her, respectively. She won the gold medal in the 100 m at the 2007 World Championships in Athletics and the gold medal in the 200 m at the 2011 World Championships in Athletics. She has also competed in the 400 m and the 800 m. During her athletics career, she has also won seven silver medals at the World Championships in Athletics. She has won two gold medals at the IAAF World Indoor Championships in the 60-meter dash over the last two years.
