Biography

Peter Michael Davidson (born November 16, 1993), addressed officially as Pete Davidson, is a humorous American actor, TV personality, and comedian.

He is most known for being a regular cast member of Saturday Night Live‘s sketch comedy program. Additionally, he has made several other TV and film appearances. 

Pete Davidson has appeared as a comedian on shows including Comedy Underground with Dave Attell, Adam DeVine’s House Party, and Jimmy Kimmel Live. The American actor backed Hillary Clinton during her presidential bids and is a tremendous admirer. 

He has a tattoo of Hillary Clinton on his leg and refers to her as his “idol.” Pete Davidson, one of the best young comedians working in the American entertainment sector today, has millions of followers all over the world. 

Pete Davidson receives a lot of appreciation for basing his comedy on his life experiences and incorporating elements from them into his acts. He frequently discusses topics like love, marijuana, college life, and other topics while playing.

Early Life & Education

Casey Davidson is the name of his younger sister. His father, a firefighter, lost his life protecting others on September 11th.

On his left arm, Pete Davidson has a tattoo of the number “8418,” which is his father’s badge number. The young youngster was severely affected by the death of his father. 

He attended Tottenville High School after finishing his studies at St. Joseph’s School. After graduating from high school, Davidson enrolled in St. Francis College, Brooklyn. However, he just briefly attended college before leaving. 

Pete Davidson was diagnosed with Crohn’s disease when he was 17 or 18 and has been on intravenous medication. The actor also utilizes medical marijuana to treat his disease-related suffering. Additionally, he has a borderline personality disorder, for which he is receiving treatment.

Career

The first time Pete Davidson appeared on screen was in an episode of the MTV series Failosophy, which debuted in February 2013. Additionally, that year, he made appearances in the television shows Guy Code, Brooklyn Nine-Nine, and Adam DeVine’s House Party, as well as the short film TubbyMan. 

The same year, Pete Davidson started appearing on Nick Cannon Presents: Wild ‘N Out. Following that, he performed stand-up in Comedy Underground and Jimmy Kimmel Live. 

He became an unchanging cast member of Saturday Night Live in 2014 and joined the ensemble. The actor also appeared in the musical movie School Dance that same year. In 2015, he appeared in the love story Trainwreck.

He participated as a roaster in the Comedy Central Roast of Justin Bieber that same year. He then made an appearance in the Comedy Central Roast of Rob Lowe shortly after. 

Pete Davidson taped Pete Davidson: SMD, his debut stand-up comedy special, in 2016. He also demonstrated his comedic abilities at that year’s The Goddamn Comedy Jam.

Personal Life

Regarding his romantic history, Pete Davidson dated Carly Aquilino from 2014 to 2015. He started dating Cazzie David after they split up, and they have been together since 2016.

The prolific has been a boyfriend to Olivia O’Brien, Ariana Grande (2018), Kate Beckinsale (2019), Margaret Qualley (2019), Kaia Gerber (2020), Phoebe Dynevor (2021), Chase Sui Wonders (2022), and Madelyn Cline (2023).

Controversially, Pete Davidson‘s girlfriend was Kim Kardashian after she divorced her ex-husband, Kanye West. The duo have been dating since October 2021 to 2022.
