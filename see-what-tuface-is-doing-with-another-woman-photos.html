A photo of popular Nigerian singer, Tuface Idibia kissing another woman other than his wife Annie Idibia, has gone viral.

Report has it that the woman is one of his other baby mamas, Pero Adeniyi, who has three children for him.

Though some of the musician’s fans have insisted it is an old photo from when 2face was still dating Pero and was only released recently by mischief makers, it can be seen from the photo that Tuface was already married as is wearing his wedding ring.

According to a report on Stella Dimokokorkus, Pero and Tuface have been in touch and are very good friends despite the fact that he got married to Annie when she (Pero) just gave birth to his 6th child, their third together.

This recent controversial photo was allegedly taken Rumours nightclub on Sunday night of January 26,2015.

A source disclosed that while Pero and Tuface were seated at the VIP section all cosy and laughing, Annie stormed the place, ready for a fight.
