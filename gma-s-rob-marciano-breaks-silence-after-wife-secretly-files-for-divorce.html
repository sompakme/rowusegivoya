GMA's Rob Marciano breaks silence after wife secretly files for divorce

 Posted on July 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  465 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The meteorologist was married for 11 years

    Good Morning America's meteorologist Rob Marciano has broken his silence after it was revealed his wife, Eryn, has filed for divorce after 11 years of marriage.

    The 54-year-old has been secretly battling heartbreak for over a year as it was revealed that Eryn filed the divorce paperwork with the Westchester, New York Supreme Court, in June 2021, according to court documents obtained by and an order for a trial was filed in May.

    Opening up about his "difficult" situation, Rob confessed that he tried to "save the marriage".

    "The last couple of years have been very difficult," Rob told in a statement. "I didn't want this and tried to save the marriage, but we are sadly divorcing. My focus now is on my kids."

    Rob and Eryn – who married in 2010 – share two children, ten-year-old daughter Madelynn and four-year-old son Mason.

    Rob and Eryn share two children

    The news of their divorce comes after they sold their family home in Rye, N.Y. for $2.7million earlier this month, according to public records obtained by .

    While their marriage may be over, the estranged couple appears to be successfully co-parenting their two children as they enjoyed a family vacation to Disney World in Orlando, Florida, in April, with Rob sharing some sweet family photos and videos on social media.

    "Spring Break @waltdisneyworld — thru the chaos was much needed kiddos quality time — their joy is as we say #magical," Rob wrote in the caption.

    The relationship between Rob and Eryn also appears to have remained amicable as shortly after she filed for divorce, Eryn posted a sweet tribute to Rob in honor of Father's Day.
