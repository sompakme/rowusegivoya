Arnold Diaz was an iconic journalist renowned for hard-hitting investigative reporting who passed away October 24th 2023, leaving an extensive legacy. Diaz became synonymous with fearless reporting which changed television journalism forever; with over five decades in his career being marked with accolades and lasting impacts both from industry peers as well as from the general public alike. His name will forever live on in television journalism’s collective memory and Diaz will live forever on through television archives around the globe.

Who Was Arnold Diaz?

Arnold Diaz first made an impactful impactful mark in television journalism with his iconic series Shame on You on WCBS-TV; its success propelled his rise and established him as an honest pursuitor. Over his long and esteemed career he earned 48 Emmy awards a testament to his talent, hard work and lasting legacy within journalism.

What Happened to Arnold Diaz?

Diaz’s career, spanning half a century, saw him at the forefront of numerous investigative reports that brought to light the malpractices in private and government sectors. After serving WCBS-TV, he extended his expertise to other notable networks like FOX 5 and ABC News, contributing significantly to ABC’s 20/20 program. In 2014, he joined PIX11 News, continuing his legacy of uncovering injustices and defending the underdog. Diaz retired in March 2022, leaving behind a legacy of fearless journalism and unwavering integrity.

How Did Arnold Diaz Die?

Arnold Diaz’s life, filled with professional triumphs, came to an end after a valiant fight against multiple myeloma cancer. His death, reported by ABC7 New York, marked the conclusion of a life dedicated to uncovering the truth and challenging the powerful.

Diaz’s battle with multiple myeloma, a type of cancer that affects plasma cells, was one he fought with the same tenacity he exhibited in his professional life. Despite his struggle with this illness, Diaz’s spirit and influence continued to resonate in the journalism community and with the countless lives he touched through his work.

Arnold Diaz’s Shame on You Program

The “Shame on You” program was more than just a segment on television; it was a crusade against injustice. Under Diaz’s stewardship, the show exposed fraudulent activities and unethical practices across various sectors. Diaz’s confrontational style, often leading him to be “shoved, spit at, and threatened with guns,” did not deter him from his mission. The program evolved in its naming — from “Shame Shame Shame” to “What a Shame!” — but its essence remained constant: giving a voice to the voiceless and holding the deceitful accountable.

The Impact of Arnold Diaz’s Work

Diaz’s investigative reports were not just journalistic achievements; they were catalysts for change. His relentless pursuit of truth led to action from law enforcement agencies and resolutions for numerous victims who had been previously ignored. His work did more than just inform; it inspired changes in policies, brought scammers to justice, and restored faith in journalism’s power to make a real difference.

Arnold Diaz’s passing marks an end of an era in investigative journalism.
