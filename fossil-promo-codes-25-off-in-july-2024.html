Yes, Fossil has free standard shipping on every order, with no minimum spend required. This will arrive within 3-5 business days. You can also choose 2-day for $20 or Overnight shipping for $30. With these options, make sure to check here for the occasional shipping codes.

Yes. New shoppers can sign up for the Fossil newsletter to get $25 off their first order of $75 or over. Simply visit the Fossil website and a ‘Get $25 off’ pop-up message will appear. You can then type your preferred email address to get your new customer code.

Does Fossil have student discounts?

At this time, there are no student discounts at Fossil. Students can still save on their watches, bags, and purses by shopping in the sale, signing up for the newsletter for $25 off their first $75 order, or using one of our Marie Claire promo codes. 

Does Fossil have military discount?

It does not appear that Fossil has a military discount currently, but if this changes we'll be the first to let you know. Alternatively, you can use one of our Marie Claire promo codes to help you save on your future purchases. 

Can I shop sales at Fossil?

Yes. Fossil has a dedicated Sales page on its website where you can save up to 50% off by shopping for discounted watches, bags, and jewelry. Fossil sales are usually seasonal, with sales such as the ‘Summer Sale’. The Outlet section also has similar Fossil products with discounted price tags. 

What is the Fossil return policy?

You can now return your Fossil order within 30 days of purchase. Any final sale, customized, or personalized products cannot be accepted for returns. You should complete the returns detail page and include this with your return. Returns shipping is free, simply print your FedEx label.
