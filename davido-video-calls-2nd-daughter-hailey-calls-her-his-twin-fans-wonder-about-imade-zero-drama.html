Nigerian singer Davido, real name David Adeleke, is back in the news after giving fans an insight into his relationship with his second daughter, Hailey.

Despite being busy, the DMW boss has made it a point of duty to always make time for family, and this wasn’t any different with his child with a US-based lady, Amanda.

Taking to his Instagram stories, Davido shared a screenshot of himself on Facetime with Hailey. Not stopping there, he went on to acknowledge the similarities in their appearance by calling his daughter his twin.
