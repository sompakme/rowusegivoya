Hogwarts Legacy Release Date, Gameplay Features, Story, and What We Know

Hogwarts Legacy Release Date: Hogwarts Legacy is one of the most anticipated video games of 2023, as it promises to let players live their full wizarding lives in the world of Harry Potter. Set in the late 1800s, the game follows a new student at Hogwarts who discovers a hidden power and a dark secret that could change the fate of the wizarding world. Here is everything we know about Hogwarts Legacy so far, from its release date and gameplay features to its story and rating.

Overview

Hogwarts Legacy Release Date

Hogwarts Legacy was originally announced in September 2020, with a planned release date of 2022. However, in January 2021, the game was delayed to 2023, as the developers stated that they needed more time to create the best possible experience for fans. The game was finally given a specific release date of February 10, 2023, for PlayStation 5, Windows, and Xbox Series X/S, during a State of Play event in March 2021.

The game will also be released on PlayStation 4 and Xbox One on May 5, 2023, and on Nintendo Switch on November 14, 2023. Players who pre-order the Deluxe Edition or the Ultimate Edition of the game will be able to access it 72 hours earlier, starting from February 7, 2023.

Gameplay Features

Hogwarts Legacy is an open-world action RPG that allows players to explore Hogwarts and its surrounding areas, such as Hogsmeade, the Forbidden Forest, and the Scottish Highlands. The game features a variety of activities and quests, such as attending classes, brewing potions, tending to magical creatures, dueling, flying, and more. Players will be able to customize their character, choose their house, and forge relationships with other students and professors.

The game also has a combat system that involves using different spells, charms, and items against various enemies, such as trolls, dark wizards, goblins, and more. The game also has a talent system that lets players upgrade their skills and abilities, such as stealth, spellcasting, and crafting. The game also has a collection system that involves finding hidden chests, solving puzzles, and unlocking pages from a field guide that contains information about the wizarding world.

Story

Hogwarts Legacy takes place in the late 1800s, a century before the events of the Harry Potter books and movies. The player takes on the role of a fifth-year student who has a unique ability to perceive and master ancient magic, a mysterious and powerful form of magic that has been hidden for centuries. The player’s character holds the key to an old secret that threatens to tear the wizarding world apart and must decide how to use their power and who to trust.

Along the way, the player will encounter various characters, both new and familiar, such as Professor Eleazar Fig, a mentor and friend who guides the player through their journey; Ranrok, a vicious goblin leader who leads a rebellion against the Ministry of Magic; Victor Rookwood, a dark wizard who has an alliance with Ranrok and a personal connection to the ancient magic; and the Keepers, a group of former Hogwarts professors who dedicated themselves to protecting the ancient magic and its secrets.

Rating

Hogwarts Legacy has been rated T for Teen by the ESRB, which means that it may contain content that is inappropriate for children under 13. According to the ESRB, the game contains blood, violence, mild language, and the use of alcohol. The game also has some dark and mature themes, such as death, torture, corruption, and betrayal. The game also allows players to make choices that will affect the outcome of the story and the fate of the characters, which may have moral and ethical implications.

Everything Else We Know

Here are some other facts and details that we know about Hogwarts Legacy:

  • The game is narrated by Jim Dale, who provided the voice for the US version of the audiobooks for the original series. Dale also narrated several earlier Harry Potter video games.
  • The game is not directly involved with J.K. Rowling, the author of the Harry Potter books, who has been criticized for her views on transgender people. However, the game does allow players to create transgender characters, according to reports.
  • The game has a variety of gear and outfits that players can obtain and wear, which reflect their house colors and preferences. The game also has a Dark Arts Pack, which is a pre-order bonus that includes a Thestral mount, a Dark Arts cosmetic set, a Dark Arts battle arena, and a Dark Arts garrison hat.
  • The game has a dynamic weather system and a day-night cycle, which affect the appearance and behavior of the environment and the creatures. The game also has a seasonal system, which changes the scenery and the activities available throughout the year.
  • The game has a multiplayer mode, which allows players to team up with their friends and other players online, and participate in various co-op and competitive activities, such as Quidditch, dueling, and exploring. The game also has a social hub, where players can chat, trade, and customize their characters.
