Ice Spice Fan Invites Her To His Graduation By Renting A Billboard

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  394 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A fan of Ice Spice has invited the New York rapper to his graduation by paying for a billboard to reach out to her. The fan, who goes by Lil Munchy Baby on Instagram, shared a picture of himself with the billboard on social media, Tuesday.

“Dear Ice Spice, I’m feelin’ u, love Munchy,” the sign reads. “You’re invited to my graduation on May 5th. Please DM.” The billboard also features Munchy in his graduation cap and holding a wad of cash. He added in the caption: “Court ordered to go to school is crazy but I did it and kept my head up high I graduated and actually got a degree finna walk the stage and I need bae there tag @icespice for me just embracing my inner munch.”

Lil Munchy Baby revealed in another post, as noted by Uproxx, that he graduated from Atlanta Metropolitan State College with a Mass Communications degree. "Only a few people know my story, truthfully music and my gang saved my life," he captioned the diploma. "You could say that I was forced to go back to school but I learned to have gratitude cuz a N***a coulda been locked up. Jersey took a charge for me my first semester of college and they still kicked me out. I owe this to my mama fasho! And she helped me realize I owe it to myself. Shit took us 11 years but we here. Thank God for everything we ain't nun without him. If you proud of me stream Pull Up today."

As for whether Ice Spice will attend, she's yet to respond to the request. The 23-year-old rapper has been blowing up over the last year since the release of her breakthrough single, "Munch (Feelin' U)." In the time since, she's gone on to reach the top 3 of the US Billboard Hot 100 with her remix with PinkPantheress, “Boy’s a Liar Pt. 2."
