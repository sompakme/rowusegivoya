Who is Kevin Gates wife Dreka Haynes?

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  828 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

RAPPER Kevin Gates is married to Dreka Haynes and the pair have spent 19 years together.

Rumors began to swirl that Kevin and Dreka were no longer together in March 2022, but there is still no confirmation of them splitting up.

Who is Kevin Gates' wife?

Kevin and Dreka started dating in 2003 before they eventually decided to tie the knot in 2015.

Dreka started to get noticed when she starred in cameos for Kevin's music videos, and fans became enamored by their relationship.

Eventually, she branched off and built her own social media platform, where she shared a look into her lifestyle.

Her Instagram has over 2.9million followers and her bio reads: "Fulfilling my purpose, being my authentic self".

Dreka told The Source in 2016, "It was crazy to see how many people knew about me for one, and were just that interested in me. I was like, 'Wow, this is insane!'

"My thing is just playing the background, but now it's like, 'No girl, you are on front street'.”

Aside from her social media presence, Dreka is the co-founder of the Bread Winner’s Association, a record label she founded with Kevin in 2010.

They founded the label after Kevin told Dreka he wanted to pursue a musical career.

Dreka told the outlet that at the time, Kevin "was always recording, but he was just doing it in a room in his grandmother's house".

But when he said he wanted to pursue it as a profession, she did her homework.

She said: "I learned any and everything I could learn about the industry on my own because nobody is just going to say, 'Hey, come on let me show you the ropes!'"

She added: "We pretty much just made our own path."

Are they still together?

Speculation began to surface in March 2022 that Dreka and her rapper husband had separated after a cryptic post on social media.

In the post, Kevin wrote, "At times, we miss our X’s but it’s in the past for a reason – do you not recall the smell, the arguments, the energy being drained, not reaching your goals, the money you could have made, the money you f**ked up, the time you should’ve invested in yourself – f**k em. #CarryOn."

It seems to become clearer in the summer of 2022 that the fans were witnessing the demise of the power couple.

On June 4, 2022, when Kevin posted a video response to a fan who asked where Dreka was.

"Man, fall this peace and all that BS, where's Dreka? Stop playing with us," the fan wrote.

Kevin responded with: "Guess I lost a fan for making myself happy."

Following the post, Kevin was seen dining at a New York City restaurant with Love and Hip Hop: Miami star Jojo Zarur on June 7, 2022.

They were seen taking photos for fans and a video shows Kevin and Zarur embracing as they walked away from fans.

Kevin released his single, Super General, on June 9, 2022, which appears to confirm their split.

The music video shows photos of Zarur as Kevin raps: "Took the blame in interviews and made it look like I was trippin'/ Made the 'Dreka' song, lied to the world while trying to protect her image."

To the surprise of fans, Dreka did not respond to the dig and later in June the couple were seen holding hands at an airport with their children.

From there, things went on quietly until they welcomed in 2023, and were spotted being intimate and celebrating together at a New Years party.

Her Instagram still remains as Dreka Gates.

Do Kevin and Dreka have children?

Kevin and Dreka have two children together - a daughter called Islah Koren, born on November 30, 2012, and their son, Khaza Kamil, born on May 10, 2014.

The couple had both children before they were married and since then, they have kept their children's lives out of the spotlight.

Dreka had a home birth for both her children which they associate with their decision to not vaccinate their children.

Kevin told Rolling Stone: "I don't believe in conspiracy theories."

Speaking out on his beliefs, Kevin said, "A lot of children that were born in a hospital experienced a lot of difficulty that they didn’t have to experience."

Dreka said her decision not to vaccinate their children was in line with Kevin's, but having a waterbirth for their children is something that she said stemmed from a near-death experience she once had in the hospital.

Dreka told The Source: "I had an incident in the hospital a few years ago where I almost died being under the care of hospital doctors.

"That was my main motivation."
