Starting off with a bang, this sequin midi dress from Amazon’s in-house brand The Drop is a no-brainer for fancy holiday parties and winter weddings. It comes in two glittery colorways — pewter and black — and is designed with cap-sleeves, a side cutout, and a slit down the back. Plus, it has a zipper closure on the back for easy on-and-off and is available in sizes XXS through 5X. Throw it on with metallic heels and a matching handbag to wow at your next event.

Another formal option, this long-sleeve mini dress is also from The Drop and comes in four colors and patterns. It has sheer mesh sleeves along with lined mesh throughout the body and flattering ruching on both the front and back. Style the dress with strappy heels and a clutch for a sexy-yet-sophisticated party look. 

If you’re looking for something a bit more low-key, consider this Zesica turtleneck sweater midi that’s equal parts comfortable and cute. It’s made from a ribbed knit material that’s available in 18 colors, and it has a body-hugging silhouette with a fabric tie around the waist for a flattering shape. As one shopper put it, the dress is “chic, comfy, and cozy” and can be “leveled up with accessories and shoes.” 

Somewhere in between a formal sequin number and a casual sweater dress falls this Merokeety velvet wrap mini. The long-sleeve dress comes in 11 colors, including seasonal jewel tones and versatile neutrals. You can dress it down with tights and flat boots, or zhuzh it up with heels and statement jewelry. Either way, you’ll “look great” and feel “comfortable all night long,” according to a reviewer.
