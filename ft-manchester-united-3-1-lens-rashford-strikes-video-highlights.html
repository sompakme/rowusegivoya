FT: Manchester United 3-1 Lens, Rashford STRIKES! [VIDEO HIGHLIGHTS]

 Posted on October 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  318 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Manchester United came from behind to beat Lens 3-1 in a preseason friendly at Old Trafford, MySportDab reports.

The game was married with the woeful performance from Mason Mount and Andre Onana who both were poor in the first half.

Thanks to Marcus Rashford, Antony and Casemiro who all scored in the second half to ensure they were able to make a clean come back.

Match Report

A shaky first half start for Manchester United saw the club concede an early goal against the visitors just few minutes into the game.

Mason Mount became the spectacle of fans after missing a glorious open net goal very early in the game which he couldn’t put into good use for his side.

Sotoca gave Lens the lead in the 23rd minute of the match after lobbing the ball from the half way line of the pitch beating Andre Onana to make it 0-1.

Alot more came to play but nothing could change the scoreline as they headed for the 45 minutes break with Manchester United 0-1 Lens.

They came in for the second half with a lot of work needed to be done as things picked up at Old Trafford for the home side moving forward.

Marcus Rashford scored in the 48th minutes of the game to make the scoreline 1-1 from a well taken goal which Manchester United made a comeback.

Another one followed in through Antony who was able to beat the Lens goalkeeper from his wonderful finish giving his side the lead 2-1 in the 54th minutes.

Casemiro made a statement for himself with a stunner scored in the 58th minutes ensuring the scoreline increased further to 3-1 as they pushed on.

There was nothing much to play as the game ended with Manchester United beating Lens 3-1 at Old Trafford after 90 minutes of football played between both teams.
