Biography

Osas was born on 26th October 1990 in New York City, USA. She is the daughter of Nigerian parents from Edo State in Nigeria. The actress has not come out to offer any information concerning the names of her parents, her upbringing and siblings, if at all she has any.

Ighodaro schooled in the United States where she attended Pennsylvania State University. She graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Broadcasting Journalism with Minors in Theater and Entrepreneurship.

Later, she enrolled at Pace University's Actors Studio Drama School, where she obtained a Master's Degree in Fine Arts.

Modelling career

Osas Ajibade started her career as a model while still in the United States. This happened immediately after winning the title of Miss Black Connecticut USA. In 2010, she won the new Miss Black USA.

Osas continued to live in the United States taking part in various modelling gigs. In 2012, she travelled to Nigeria with a plan of staying for six months.
