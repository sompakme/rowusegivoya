Demi and Ashton in love in Beverly Hills

 Posted on September 17, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  197 words  |  Larita Shotwell

    As the one-year anniversary of the start of their romance approaches, Hollywood star Demi Moore and her younger beau, actor Ashton Kutcher, are still going strong.

    The happy couple looked like two teenagers in love as they held hands at a Beverly Hills bash celebrating the career of former Gucci designer Tom Ford on Sunday.

    Demi was all smiles as she looked adoringly up at her 6ft 3in boyfriend, who towered over the petite brunette despite her gold high-heeled shoes. And the 15-year age difference between the pair, who began dating last May, was barely noticeable, with the 41-year-old actress looking sexier than ever in a form-fitting cocktail dress.

    But Bruce Willis' ex-wife wasn't the only head-turning Tinseltown beauty cheering on designer Ford when he picked up the Rodeo Drive Walk Of Style award, however. Demi's Charlie's Angels Full Throttle co-star Lucy Liu, Married With Children's Christina Applegate and English rose Kate Beckinsale were also on hand at the event.
