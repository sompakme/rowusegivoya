The last seemed not to be heard about the fight for justice for late singer, Ilerioluwa Imole Aloba, professionally known as Mohbad.

Legit.ng had reported that Joseph Aloba, father of the late singer, insisted that a DNA test must be done to determine the paternity of his grand child, Liam.

In a video sighted online, the musician claimed his son was killed because of DNA.

Aloba insists on DNA

In the recording, the late Mohbad, who just released a song, said that if a DNA was done, the secret of his son's death would be exposed.

Speaking further, he noted that he had the right as the singer's father to ask for a DNA for his grandson.

Aloba blasts his ex-wife

Also in the video, Aloba accused his ex-wife, who is Mohbad's mother, of trying to reap where she didn't sow.

He said his ex-wife didn't know anything about their son, the schools he attended, but was just claiming to have checked on him in school.
