A sad couple has sued a hospital in court for switching their baby when she was born.

According to a Tweet by Saint Zoe, the father had conducted a DNA test, only to discover that his daughter wasn't biologically his.

He accused his wife of infidelity, but she swore she had never cheated on him since they were together.

The wife decided to carry out her test, which proved that she was also not her daughter's biological mother.
