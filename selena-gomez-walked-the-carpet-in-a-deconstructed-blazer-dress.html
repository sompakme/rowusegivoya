Selena Gomez Walked the Carpet in a Deconstructed Blazer Dress

 Posted on September 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  340 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Selena Gomez went sexy-meets-corporate last night while celebrating her beauty brand, Rare Beauty.

The "Look at Her Now" singer attended the launch of Rare Beauty's Kind Words Matte Lipstick and Liner Collection at the Santa Monica Proper Hotel in California last night.

She happily posed for photos on the red carpet wearing a boxy, deconstructed black blazer minidress with sheer lace detailing down one side from Alexander McQueen's autumn/winter 2022 collection.

She finished the elevated look with black sky-high strappy sandals and big gold hoop earrings by Monica Vinader. For beauty, Gomez went for her usual black winged liner and red lip, and wore her hair down and styled straight.

Presley Ann//Getty Images

"I wanted to create a classic lipstick that's truly comfortable. Every shade is easy and wearable, and just perfect for every day," Gomez told BAZAAR.com last week about her exciting new collection.

The lip liners also come in 10 shades that match back to the lipsticks, and they all come with a built-in sharpener. "This is everything I've ever wanted in a lip liner. Super smooth and doesn't tug on your lips and made to last all day without feeling sticky," she said.

Gomez shared a video from the event on her Instagram Story to thank her fans and reveal that the new products would be available to shop online for 24 hours only, prior to their official launch on July 7.

Presley Ann//Getty Images

Apart from running Rare Beauty, the singer, actress, and entrepreneur has been working on crime-comedy series Only Murders in the Building, as well as running her mental wellness platform, Wondermind, which she spoke exclusively to BAZAAR about earlier this year.
