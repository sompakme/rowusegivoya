How to Reset the Air Bag Light on a Nissan Armada

Insert the key into the ignition and turn the key to the “On” position, being careful not to start the engine while doing so.

Wait for the SRS light to begin blinking before proceeding. As soon as the light stops blinking, switch off the car and remove the ignition key from the ignition.

Replace the ignition key and repeat steps 1 and 2 until the battery is fully charged.

Steps 1 and 2 should be repeated three more times.
