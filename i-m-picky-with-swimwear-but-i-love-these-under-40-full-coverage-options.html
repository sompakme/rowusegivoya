I'm Picky With Swimwear, but I Love These Under-$40 Full-Coverage Options

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  360 words  |  Martina Birk

Whenever I go swimsuit shopping, I have a hard time finding options that completely cover my backside. Most styles these days are especially cheeky, and while that works for some people, it's just not my vibe. That's why I went on a hunt for full-coverage swimsuits, and I found a few that have earned permanent spots in my wardrobe — all for under $40.

You'll find both one-piece swimsuits and bikinis with full-coverage bottoms available in a wide range of sizes. Keep scrolling through to check out the under-$40 swimsuits you need to get your wardrobe ready for summer.

Shop Full-Coverage Swimsuits Under $40

Cupshe High-Waist Bikini Set

A top-rated bikini option, the Cupshe High-Waist Set comes in four color combinations and sizes XXS through XXL. It includes a ruffled top with adjustable straps and a pair of high-waisted bottoms with piping on the front. One Amazon shopper who was "expecting either the top or bottom to not fit" was pleasantly surprised that both pieces "fit perfectly," and they even went back to order more colors.

Hilor Crossover One-Piece Swimsuit

With 370 five-star ratings, the Hilor Crossover One-Piece Swimsuit is clearly a favorite among Amazon shoppers. It comes in an impressive 45 colors and patterns and sizes 4 through 16. The popular one-piece has cross-front straps, cutouts around the neckline and across the waist with mesh underneath, and removable pads in the bust. Fun fact: On a beach vacation last week, I saw at least five women wearing this swimsuit in different colors, and now it's in my cart.

Cupshe Scallop Trim One-Piece Swimsuit

A cutout moment, the Cupshe Scallop Trim One-Piece Swimsuit comes in four colors, each with a diamond-shaped pattern woven into the fabric and scalloped edges. It has adjustable straps and slight cutouts under the bust and on the back. As one reviewer said, it's made from "good quality, thicker material." And after seeing my friend wear this suit last week, I can confirm that to be true.
