FAQs

Does Zappos have free shipping?

Yes. Zappos has free standard shipping for each order with no minimum order total. When you sign up for the Zappos VIP rewards program or download their app, you can qualify for free expedited shipping.  

How often do Zappos have sales?

Zappos has a sale section that you can shop on their website all year round. They also hold seasonal sales throughout the year on their website during seasonal periods like summer and winter sales, as well as key dates such as Memorial Day and Black Friday.  

Is there a Zappos rewards program?

Yes and it's free to join! When you join Zappos VIP, you'll earn 1 point for every $1 you spend, access to free expedited shipping, and extra perks such as Amazon Prime members earning extra points. 

Can I get a military discount at Zappos?

Unfortunately, Zappos doesn't currently offer a military discount. Previously, military personnel could receive a 10% Zappos promo code and, should this become available again, we will update our information to give you access to the best savings. 

What is the Zappos return policy?

Zappos has free returns for customers in the US with a 365-day return policy. Your item should be unworn and in its original packaging. Final Sale items cannot be returned. Be sure to check the terms and conditions on the Zappos website before making your purchase.
