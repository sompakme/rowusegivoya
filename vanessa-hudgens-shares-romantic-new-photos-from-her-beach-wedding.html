Vanessa Hudgens Shares Romantic New Photos From Her Beach Wedding

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  365 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Vanessa Hudgens is in newlywed bliss, and we’re here for it.

Over the weekend, the High School Musical star shared beautiful new photos on Instagram from her dreamy beach wedding to Cole Tucker. In them, she is seen posing on the beach in Tulum, Mexico, in a breezy summer dress. The off-the-shoulder Dean dress, by Galia Lahav, features pretty ribbons on the straps, a chest cutout, a ruched, corseted bodice, a high leg slit, and a big, flowy skirt.

Hudgens wore it with gold jewelry, including Bottega Veneta’s viral gold drop earrings, and bronzy, glowy makeup. Her hair was slicked back into a neat bun.

“She’s a wife now,” Hudgens captioned the photos.

The actor debuted the romantic, bohemian dress for a welcome dinner that kicked off her and Tucker's wedding weekend. The pair married last Saturday, December 2, at Azulik, a fantastical tribal-concept resort in Tulum.

Hudgens and MLB player Tucker began dating in late 2020 after meeting on a Zoom meditation group call, months after the actor’s split from actor Austin Butler, whom she dated for more than eight years. In November 2021, the couple made their red carpet debut at the premiere of her film Tick, Tick… Boom! And this February, the free-agent baseball star proposed.

In an Instagram post announcing their engagement, Hudgens shared a photo of herself and Tucker looking happy with the Eiffel Tower behind them, as well as a snap of her hand with her new diamond ring. “YES. We couldn’t be happier 🤍,” she wrote in the caption.

She later posted a photo of Tucker kissing her cheek at their engagement party. “Real magnetic love is out there. What a journey it was to get here but so worth it to get to my forever @cotuck 🥰 Happy Valentine’s Day❤️,” she wrote in the caption.
