Following the demise of Mr Ibu, Pastor Robert Jr's prophecy about the comic actor has resurfaced online.

Robert Jr, the senior prophet of Grace Confirmation Church Worldwide, Enugu, had on December 31, 2023, released a series of prophecies about future happenings in 2024.

A church member, Kasiemobi Obim, revisited her Facebook post of January 2 containing the 42 prophecies Robert Jr released to confirm that of Mr Ibu.

Tinubu's minister mourns Mr Ibu's demise

Meanwhile, Legit.ng previously reported that Tinubu's minister had mourned the death of comic actor Mr Ibu.

Hannatu Musawa, minister of art, culture and creative economy, has described the death of veteran actor, John Okafor, popularly known as Mr Ibu, as one death too many.

The President Bola Ahmed Tinubu's appointee lamented that Mr Ibu’s death came just 24 hours after another Nollywood comic actor, Tolani Quadri Oyebamiji, commonly called Sisi Quadri, passed away.
